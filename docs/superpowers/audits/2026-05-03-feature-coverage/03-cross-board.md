## Special call-out: does the plan adequately cover the 28 %-of-outbound cross-board send pattern?

**Partially yes, with two notable gaps.**

**What the plan covers (K.1–K.3 substantively):**

1. Plan step **2.3.j** (Post-provisioning send + ACL refresh, Discovery 14): seeds ~784 `agent_destinations` rows (28 × 27) at cutover and calls `writeDestinations(parent_id, sess_id)` after every `provision_taskflow_board`. This handles K.1 (the named-ACL substitute for v1's free-for-all `taskflowManaged↔taskflowManaged` grant) and K.2 (per-pair authorization).
2. Plan step **2.3.c** (`taskflow_send_message_with_audit` wrapper): writes a log row immediately after `writeMessageOut` with `delivery_status='queued'`. This handles K.3 (audit trail).
